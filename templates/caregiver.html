How SNS Alerts Work
  1. An SNS Topic is created in your AWS account.
  2. Your caregiver subscribes to the topic via email or SMS.
  3. When a dose is missed, the scheduler publishes a message to the topic.
  4. AWS SNS delivers the alert to all subscribers instantly.
Configure SNS_TOPIC_ARN in your .env file to enable alerts.
